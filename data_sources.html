    NOAA

    • Operational Hydro-Estimator Satellite Rainfall Estimates
      • Description: rainfall rate estimates every 15 minutes for CONUS using GEOS and every 30 minutes globally using METEOSAT & MTSAT; includes instantaneous rates and 1-hour, 3-hour, 6-hour, 24-hour and multi-day accumulations
      • AWS netcdf archive (2019-present): NOAA Global Hydro Estimator (GHE) - Registry of Open Data on AWS
      • Azure netcdf archive: NOAA Global Hydro Estimator | AIforEarthDataSets
    • [National Blend of Models - MDL - Virtual Lab](https://vlab.noaa.gov/web/mdl/nbm
