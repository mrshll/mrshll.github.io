Me

You can call me Marsh or Marshall. I was born in Hoboken, raised in Nashville. I lived in Boston for 15 years before moving back to Nashville in late 2024. I'm married to Alejandra and parent of Meadow (human) and Daxter (pup).

I started and now run a climate and conservation company, {Upstream Tech} (see {Work and Pursuits of Toil} for a ... complete history). I love programming languages and system design challenges.

I run and climb. Make {music}, {Garden}, and play games with my childhood friends.

I like turning everyday things into an adventure, like a commute to work with {Early Werms} or {walking up a river}. I try to run a slow, unplanned, solo marathon once a year. I once reviewed milk on {Milk Advocate} with my friend Quinault (and then became almost vegan, now undone because of baby!).
