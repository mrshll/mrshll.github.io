      Notes

      Finding someone to talk to:
    • select a frequency in the range ((insert range))
    • Listen to see if anyone else is on the frequency
    • Ask if the frequency is in use "This is AAAAA, is this frequency clear for me to call CQ?"

    Q codes: all of em here

    • QRM: interference from other stations ("I'm hearing QRM on your signal")
    • QTH: location
    • QSL: a conversation
    • QSY: changing frequency or channel

    The formal definition of 73 is “Best Regards” – it's a nice way to say goodbye that is unique to amateur radio.
